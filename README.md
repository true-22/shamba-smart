      <p>Shamba Smart is a mobile-first web application that allows smallholder farmers in Kenya to photograph a crop leaf and instantly receive a disease diagnosis with treatment recommendations all in under 5 seconds, directly in their phone's browser with no app installation required.</p>
      <p><strong>Live App:</strong> <a href="https://huggingface.co/spaces/True-22/shamba-smart">https://huggingface.co/spaces/True-22/shamba-smart</a></p>
      <p>   </p>
      <h4>How to Access It</h4>
      <p>1. Open your phone's browser (Chrome recommended on Android)</p>
      <p>2. Go to: https://huggingface.co/spaces/True-22/shamba-smart</p>
      <p>3. Allow camera access when prompted</p>
      <p>4. Point your camera at a diseased crop leaf and tap Scan Leaf</p>
      <p>5. Wait 3–5 seconds for the AI diagnosis</p>

Best results on: Android phones running Chrome. Also works on iPhone Safari and on laptop/desktop browsers (via file upload instead of camera).

First visit note: 
The app may take 30–60 seconds to load on the very first visit after a period of inactivity. This is normal as Hugging Face free-tier Spaces sleep when unused and wake up on the next visit. Subsequent scans are fast.
